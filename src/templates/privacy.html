Privacy Policy

This document describes how we process and store your information.

We do not collect or store any personal information as defined by PIPEDA.

However, we do collect other types of information both voluntarily provided by you or transmitted to us by your browser. Those include but are not limited to your browser's user-agent, your IP address, your submissions, and your email. We safeguard your data with industry-standard security practices, and will only analyze your data to investigate errors or abuse. We will never sell or give away your data.

We do not track your data through trackers like Google Analytics. We also do not deploy any cookies, except for those that are required for site functionality (such as keeping you logged in).
